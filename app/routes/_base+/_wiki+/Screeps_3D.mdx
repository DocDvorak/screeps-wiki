title: Screeps 3D
---

[Screep's 3D](https://github.com/thmsndk/Screeps3D/releases) is a 3D client for Screeps created by [bonzaiferroni](https://github.com/bonzaiferroni/Screeps3D) and is maintained [thmsndk](https://github.com/thmsndk/Screeps3D) along with the Screeps3D community.

You can read some of the history of its creation via the [way-back machine](https://web.archive.org/web/20180816225435/http://screeps.high5r.com/making-a-screeps-client/), or stay up-to-date on the #screeps3d channel in [slack](/Community_Communication).
